Our story

FM Kitchen + Bar is a chef-driven concept that offers a contemporary take on southern classics, gourmet burgers, and craft cocktails. Inspired by his time growing up in Texas, Executive Chef and co-owner Ryan Hildebrand applies his fine dining background and techniques to the southern regional dishes of his youth. With the same attention-to-detail and quality established by the team at Triniti, FM Kitchen + Bar’s delicious offerings are executed at the highest level, while still being affordable and approachable, maintaining the welcoming feel of a timeless neighborhood joint. After all, it started with a burger!
